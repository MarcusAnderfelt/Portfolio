About Me

I’m Marcus Anderfelt, a passionate Game Designer and Level Designer with a focus on creating engaging gameplay systems and immersive environments.

I enjoy combining mechanics, storytelling, and visual design to craft memorable player experiences - wether that's through puzzle design, multiplayer systems, or worldbuilding tools.

My experience ranges from level design and narrative-driven puzzle gameplay (as in Overstay) to mechanics design and blueprint programming in Unreal Engine 5 (as in Imagmination). I also explore the visual side of game design, working with style guides and concept art to communicate ideas clearly and creatively.

I thrive in collaborative team enivornments, contributing both to the design vision and the polish and balance of projects. At the same time, i'm comfortable working independently on creative projects, from concept to final presentation.

When I'm not working on games, I'm constantly sketching, testing mechanics, and experimenting with new tools - always looking for ways to improve my craft as a designer.

Skills: Unreal Engine, Blueprints, Photoshop, Illustrator, Team Collaboration
